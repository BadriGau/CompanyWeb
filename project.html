Company XYZ

The company XYZ in the current situation decided to implement new work measures to potentiate social distancing: allow the requisition of equipment necessary for the performance of employees tasks.

Implemented Features:

  • Login for all company employees;
  • Logout;
  • Ordered list of information stored in the database about departments, employees, equipment category and equipment;
  • List of employees of a certain department;
  • Equipment list requested from a certain employee;
  • List of equipment of a certain category;
  • Insert new data for departments, employees, equipment and equipment category;
  • Eliminate the observations inserted in a specific equipment.
